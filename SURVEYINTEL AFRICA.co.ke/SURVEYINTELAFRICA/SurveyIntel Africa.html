ABOUT SURVEYINTEL AFRICA

Bridging mathematics with geospatial technology for unparalleled accuracy.

SurveyIntel Africa was founded by Kepha Njenga, a registered land surveyor with a unique background in Pure Mathematics. This combination enables us to approach spatial challenges with both technical precision and analytical rigor.

Our team of certified professionals uses state-of-the-art equipment including RTK GPS, robotic total stations, and advanced GNSS processing software to deliver results you can trust.
